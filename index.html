yt-pipeline

A private, single-user automation tool for one YouTube channel.

yt-pipeline is a personal automation tool operated by an individual creator. It publishes videos produced by its owner to the owner's own YouTube channel and reads that same channel's analytics to improve future content. It does not access, store, or process data belonging to any other YouTube user.

What it does

  • Uploads finished videos to the owner's own YouTube channel.
  • Manages the metadata (titles, descriptions, scheduling) of those videos.
  • Reads the owner's own channel performance metrics (views, retention, CTR).
